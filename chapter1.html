गणित मंजरी (Mathematics)

Chapter 1 - Coordinate Geometry (निर्देशांक ज्यामिति)

1. Introduction

Coordinate Geometry is the branch of mathematics that helps us locate the position of points using numbers.

2. Coordinate Plane

A coordinate plane is formed when two number lines intersect each other at right angles.

3. X-Axis and Y-Axis

  • The X-axis is horizontal.
  • The Y-axis is vertical.
  • Both axes meet at the origin.

4. Origin

The origin is the point where the X-axis and Y-axis intersect. Its coordinates are (0,0).

5. Quadrants

  • First Quadrant : (+,+)
  • Second Quadrant : (-,+)
  • Third Quadrant : (-,-)
  • Fourth Quadrant : (+,-)

6. Ordered Pair

An ordered pair is written as (x, y). It represents the position of a point on the coordinate plane.

7. Plotting Points

To plot the point (3,2):

  • Move 3 units to the right on the X-axis.
  • Move 2 units upward parallel to the Y-axis.
  • Mark the point.

8. Important Facts

  • Origin = (0,0)
  • On the X-axis, y = 0
  • On the Y-axis, x = 0
  • Coordinates are always written as (x,y)

9. Practice Questions

  1. What are the coordinates of the origin?
  2. In which quadrant does the point (4,5) lie?
  3. In which quadrant does the point (-3,2) lie?
  4. Write the difference between the X-axis and Y-axis.

10. Summary

Coordinate Geometry helps us locate points on a plane using ordered pairs. The X-axis, Y-axis, Origin, and Quadrants are its main concepts.

10. सारांश (Summary)

निर्देशांक ज्यामिति में बिंदुओं की स्थिति को (x,y) के रूप में व्यक्त किया जाता है। X-अक्ष, Y-अक्ष, Origin तथा Quadrants इसके मुख्य भाग हैं।
