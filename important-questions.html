Q6. Are the points M(-3,-4), A(0,0) and G(6,8) on the same straight line?
Slope of MA = 4/3
Slope of AG = 8/6 = 4/3

Since both slopes are equal, the points lie on the same straight line.

Answer: Yes, M, A and G are collinear.
Q7. Check whether R(-5,-1), B(-2,-5) and C(4,-12) are on the same straight line.
Slope of RB = -4/3
Slope of BC = -7/6

The slopes are not equal.

Answer: No, the points are not collinear.
